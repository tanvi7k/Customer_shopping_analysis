**Project 1: Sales & Customer Behavior Analysis Dashboard**
**Project Objective**

The objective of this project is to analyze sales and customer behavior data to identify trends, patterns, and key performance indicators (KPIs) that help improve business decision-making and drive sales growth.

**Tools & Technologies Used**

Microsoft Excel
Power BI
Data Cleaning & Transformation
Pivot Tables
Data Visualization
Dashboard Design
Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

**Dataset Description**

The dataset contains historical sales and customer information, including:
Order details
Customer demographics (age, gender, location)
Sales valus
Product categories
Sales channels
Order status

**Key Business Questions (KPIs)**

Compare total sales and total orders using a single visualization
Identify the month with the highest sales and order volume
Analyze purchasing behavior by gender
Determine order status distribution (delivered, cancelled, returned, etc.)
Identify top 10 states contributing to total sales
Analyze relationship between age group and gender based on order count
Identify sales contribution by different channels
Find the highest selling product category
Calculate percentage of successfully delivered orders

**Data Cleaning & Preparation
**
Removed missing and duplicate values
Standardized data formats (dates, numbers, categories)
Verified data consistency and accuracy
Created calculated fields and measures for analysis

**Data Analysis Process**

Defined business objectives and KPIs
Cleaned and transformed raw dat
Created pivot tables and measures
Built interactive visualizations in Power BI
Designed a dynamic dashboard using slicers and filters
Analyzed trends and patterns
Derived actionable insights

**Dashboard Features**

Interactive Power BI dashboard
Slicers for dynamic filtering
Multiple charts for KPI tracking
Clear visual representation of trends and insights

**Key Insights**

Female customers contribute a higher percentage of total sales
Specific states contribute significantly to overall revenue
Middle-aged customers generate the highest number of orders
Online marketplaces are the primary sales channels
High percentage of orders are successfully delivered

**Business Impact
**
Helps stakeholders understand customer behavior
Identifies high-value customer segments
Supports data-driven marketing strategie
Improves sales planning and inventory decisions

**Conclusion**

This project demonstrates strong skills in data analysis, visualization, and business insight generation. The interactive dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor performance, identify growth opportunities, and make informed decisions to improve overall business outcomes.
